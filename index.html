STICKER GUIDE

★ APPLICATION INSTRUCTIONS ★

Sticker & Sunstrip Application Guide

All Obsessed Brand UK stickers are made using premium vinyl, precision-cut on our Graphtec cutters, and topped with easy-apply transfer tape for a clean install every time.

Vinyl Sticker Application

1. Prep the surface
Thoroughly clean and dry the area with IPA (isopropyl rubbing alcohol). Do not use wax-based products, detailing sprays, or sealants — vinyl will not stick properly to treated surfaces. Make sure the surface is completely dry before moving on.

2. Transfer the decal
Slowly peel the backing paper away from the transfer tape at a sharp angle. If parts of the decal don't lift, rub the tape firmly with a flat object (a credit card works well) until it does.

3. Apply & smooth out
Position the decal where you want it. Firmly press it down, running a card over the surface to push out any bubbles.

4. Remove the tape
Leave the decal for 10–20 minutes to adhere. Carefully peel away the transfer tape at a sharp angle. If any part of the decal lifts, press it back down with your card and peel again slowly.

Sunstrip Application (Universal Fit)

Our sunstrips are universal size and require trimming once fitted. All air release sunstrips must be applied dry — never wet apply.

STEP 1 – Prep the windscreen
Clean thoroughly with IPA and a clean microfiber cloth. Remove all wax, Rain-X, glass sealants, or residues — they stop vinyl from sticking. Do not use household glass cleaners. Make sure the surface is 100% dry before moving on.

STEP 2 – Measure twice, fit once
Offer the sunstrip up to the glass and secure with masking tape. Use a tape measure and symmetrical points for perfect placement. Create a vertical tape hinge down the middle.

STEP 3 – Apply one side
Peel the backing paper away up to the center hinge. Cut the backing neatly with scissors (don't tear). Hold the vinyl taut and apply.

STEP 4 – Squeegee time
Using a felt squeegee, press the vinyl from the center outwards. Apply firm pressure and work out bubbles as you go. Air release vinyl is forgiving — bubbles can be pushed out.

STEP 5 – Repeat for the other side
Remove the hinge tape, peel the remaining backing, and apply as before.

STEP 6 – Trim safely
Squeegee right up to the glass edges. Trim excess carefully with a sharp craft knife — avoid seals or paint. Remove the protective transfer tape if present.

Helpful Tips

Apply in cool, dry conditions — not too hot, not too cold (below 10°C for standard vinyl / below 15°C for special effects vinyl will cause failures). Take your time — decals are not reusable once applied. Adhesive takes 48–72 hours to fully cure. Avoid jet washing or high-pressure cleaning during this time.

Colour Disclaimer: Actual shades may vary slightly. Screen settings and monitor calibration mean colours shown online are for guidance only.

Washing & Care

Hoodies & T-Shirts: Machine wash cold (30°C max) inside out. Do not tumble dry — lay flat or hang dry to preserve the print. Do not iron directly on the print. Do not bleach.

Personalised Items: Extra care is needed for personalised prints. Always wash inside out, cold wash only. Avoid fabric softener as it can degrade printed areas over time.

Soft Shell Jackets: Machine wash cold on a gentle cycle. Do not tumble dry. Re-spray with a DWR (Durable Water Repellent) treatment periodically to maintain waterproofing.

★ OUR RETURNS POLICY ★

Returns & Refunds Policy

We want you to love your purchase, but if something isn't right, here's how our returns work.

Return Window

You have 28 days from the purchase date to return your item. After 28 days, we cannot offer a refund or exchange.

Eligibility

To qualify for a return, your item must be unused, unworn, and unwashed, in the same condition as received with original packaging and tags, free from stains, pet/human hair, holes, tears, or strong odours. Personalised or made-to-order products are not eligible unless an error was made on our end.

Clothing: Must be complete with tags and in re-sellable condition.
Stickers: Backing must be intact and unused.

Non-Returnable Items

Gift cards, personalised items, made-to-order items, and heat transfer store items (covered by an 8-week warranty from purchase date only) cannot be returned.

Faulty or Incorrect Items

If your order arrives faulty or incorrect, notify us within 48 hours by emailing clvinyldesigns@hotmail.com with photographic evidence. We will replace the item free of charge and cover return postage costs.

Postage

Customers are responsible for return postage unless the item is faulty or incorrect. Always request proof of postage or use recorded delivery. If a return is lost without proof, we cannot process a refund.

Refunds

Approved returns will be refunded once received and inspected. Partial refunds may apply if the item is not in original condition, is damaged or missing parts not due to our error, or is returned more than 30 days after delivery.

Shipping Policy 🚚

Orders are usually packed and shipped within 1–5 working days. Personalised orders may take up to 10 working days. During busy periods, dispatch times may be longer. Need your order quickly? Contact us before placing your order and we'll confirm current dispatch times.

⚠️ Dispatch time ≠ shipping time. Shipping time is how long Royal Mail / DPD takes after dispatch.

We ship worldwide. Delivery times vary depending on the destination: Europe is usually 5–7 working days, and the rest of the world is typically 3–4 weeks. Customers are responsible for paying all import costs such as VAT, duties, and clearance fees. For international orders that haven't arrived within 4 weeks, please get in touch.

Shipping Costs

Our shipping prices are based on official Royal Mail rates, calculated by size and weight. We keep our prices as competitive as possible. Orders over £30 qualify for free UK shipping.

Address & Delivery Issues

Please ensure your delivery address is complete and correct. If Royal Mail attempts delivery and you miss it, your parcel will be held at your local sorting office. We receive proof of delivery for every order.
